The business is run by Mr John Mwangi Njuguna (the proprietor), his wife Mrs Mercy Muthoni, and a staff of twelve permanent and six casual staff. Baseline Stores has a fleet of four vehicles for delivery of goods to customers as well as procurement of goods from various suppliers. Mr Njuguna is a Kenya Utalii graduate with very good knowledge of wines and spirits from different regions across the globe. He has been in the wines and spirits business for the past fifteen years and therefore has a lot of experience in this field. He is the proud owner of a thriving business that supplies to a wide range of hotels serving as the main client√®le.

We aim to:
  • To provide quality, reasonably priced wines and spirits to meet the demands of our customers.
  • To be the leading suppliers of wines and spirits in Naivasha and other towns.
  • To supply in shortest time possible and also act fast when emergencies arise.
  • To ensure compliance with statutory requirements and procedures.

Customer satisfaction is our priority. Baseline stores believe that good relationships with stake holders is a prerequisite to our success. We also believe in abiding with the law of land in the sale of licensed wines and spirits. We intend to improve our turnovers by 25% this year while at the same time maintaining low expenditures on our operations. We expect our customer base to grow by 15% in order to achieve our desired growth.
